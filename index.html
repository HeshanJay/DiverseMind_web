Introduction

In todayâs competitive business landscape, streamlining the hiring process is crucial for attracting and retaining top talent efficiently. Traditional recruitment methods often result in delays and mismatches, leading to higher turnover and costs. To address these challenges, companies are adopting AI technologies like Natural Language Processing (NLP), machine learning, and chatbots to improve recruitment speed and accuracy.

Our team developed an AI-powered recruitment system that enhances the efficiency and accuracy of hiring. Using machine learning and NLP, it automates CV screening, skill assessment, and candidate-job matching, streamlining the recruitment process.

Abstraction

The recruitment process in many organizations is often slow and inefficient, struggling to match candidates with job openings effectively. This research presents an AI-powered system that enhances the accuracy and efficiency of the hiring process by leveraging natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms. The system automates key stages of recruitment, including CV shortlisting, skill assessment, personality analysis, and job recommendations. It features an NLP-based CV shortlisting tool, skill evaluation quizzes, a chatbot for candidate-HR interactions, and an AI bot for document verification. By streamlining recruitment and improving candidate-job matching, the system offers a scalable solution to reduce hiring time and effort while increasing accuracy. Future improvements will focus on algorithm refinement, expanded functionality, and real-world validation before commercialization.
